Inside the weighting engine

Every ask is scored on the same scale before anyone argues about priority.

Sales / partnerships
  • Cares about revenue — deal value, likelihood, timeline
  • A weight on the engine, not a bypass
Product
  • Cares about the vision — a coherent roadmap
  • Features that generalize, not one-off builds
Engineering
  • Cares about stability & focus — effort and interruption cost
  • The sprint is the rhythm
The scoring variables
  • Impact — what does it move?
  • Urgency — what does waiting cost?
  • Breadth — one client, or a pattern?
  • Effort — what does it cost to build?
  • Contract or revenue on the line?
  • Workaround — or dead in the water?

Argue at the score, not the decision. One visible queue. Nothing reaches the broker unweighed.

The important parts
    How it feeds tier 3
      The standard — the credibility model

      Every escalation should save an engineer time, not create work. The whole posture is making their lives easier — never pretending you can do their job. Run every step on this standard, and engineering ends up thanking you:

      • Be a load reducer, not a load adder — every interaction costs focus or saves it
      • Do your own triage first, every time
      • Never make them chase you — they should never have to ask twice
      • Admit the edge of your knowledge
      • Give the credit outward when it ships
      • Build the docs and runbooks with them, so every hand-off gets lighter
